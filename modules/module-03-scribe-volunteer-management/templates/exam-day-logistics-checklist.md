# Exam Day Logistics Checklist: Step-by-Step Coordination Guide

## Pre-Examination Preparation (24-48 Hours Before)

### Final Volunteer Confirmation

**[ ] Send confirmation reminders** to all assigned scribes via WhatsApp/SMS
- Include examination date, time, venue, and student contact information
- Request immediate response confirming availability
- Provide backup coordinator contact numbers for emergencies

**[ ] Activate backup protocols** for any non-responsive volunteers
- Contact backup volunteers within 2 hours of non-response
- Confirm backup volunteer availability and briefing needs
- Update scheduling database with final assignments

**[ ] Student notification** confirming scribe arrangements
- Send student confirmation with scribe name and contact information
- Provide examination venue details and arrival time recommendations
- Share coordinator contact for any last-minute issues

### Materials and Documentation Preparation

**[ ] Prepare scribe assignment packets** for each examination
- Volunteer contact information and student pairing details
- Examination venue, date, time, and duration information
- Special accommodation requirements or instructions
- Emergency contact numbers and backup procedures

**[ ] Print required forms** (if not using digital systems)
- Scribe declaration forms for signature
- Attendance tracking sheets
- Post-examination feedback forms
- Emergency contact reference sheets

**[ ] Coordinate with examination administrators**
- Confirm scribe accommodation arrangements with exam supervisors
- Review seating arrangements and accessibility requirements
- Clarify any special protocols or institutional requirements
- Provide contact information for coordination support

## Day of Examination: Morning Preparation (2-3 Hours Before)

### Coordinator Pre-Check

**[ ] Review daily schedule** and volunteer assignments
- Confirm all examinations have confirmed scribe coverage
- Check for any schedule changes or last-minute modifications
- Prepare for potential emergency coordination needs

**[ ] Test communication systems**
- Ensure mobile phone is fully charged with backup power
- Verify WhatsApp groups are active and accessible
- Test backup communication methods (email, alternate phone)

**[ ] Prepare emergency resources**
- Identify backup volunteers available for emergency calls
- Confirm transportation options for emergency volunteer deployment
- Prepare emergency contact lists for students and staff

### Early Morning Coordination (1-2 Hours Before)

**[ ] Send morning reminders** to all volunteers and students
- Confirm examination start times and arrival expectations
- Remind volunteers to arrive 15 minutes early for briefing
- Provide real-time coordinator contact information

**[ ] Coordinate with examination venues**
- Confirm room accessibility and seating arrangements
- Review any special equipment or materials requirements
- Establish communication protocols with examination supervisors

**[ ] Monitor for last-minute issues**
- Check for volunteer cancellations or transportation problems
- Prepare for potential student accommodation changes
- Coordinate with backup volunteers for emergency deployment

## Volunteer Arrival and Briefing (30-60 Minutes Before)

### Volunteer Check-In Process

**[ ] Verify volunteer identity** and assignment details
- Confirm volunteer name matches assignment records
- Review student pairing and examination details
- Check volunteer training certification status

**[ ] Conduct brief orientation** for venue and procedures
- Show seating arrangements and accessibility features
- Review examination timing and break procedures
- Confirm emergency protocols and coordinator contact

**[ ] Distribute materials** and complete documentation
- Provide pens, paper, and any required materials
- Complete scribe declaration forms and confidentiality agreements
- Review specific accommodation requirements for assigned student

### Student-Scribe Introduction

**[ ] Facilitate introductory meeting** between student and scribe
- Allow 5-10 minutes for brief conversation and comfort building
- Review any student-specific communication preferences
- Confirm mutual understanding of examination procedures

**[ ] Review accommodation details** specific to student needs
- Confirm time allocation and compensation time requirements
- Review any assistive technology or materials being used
- Clarify communication methods and preference protocols

**[ ] Address any concerns** or questions from either party
- Provide coordinator contact information for issues during examination
- Confirm both parties understand their roles and responsibilities
- Document any special arrangements or modifications needed

## During Examination: Active Monitoring

### First 30 Minutes

**[ ] Monitor examination start** and initial setup
- Confirm all scribes are present and properly positioned
- Address any immediate seating or logistics issues
- Verify examination materials distribution is complete

**[ ] Check for early issues** requiring intervention
- Monitor for communication problems between students and scribes
- Address any technology or materials problems quickly
- Coordinate with examination supervisors for any needed adjustments

**[ ] Maintain communication availability** for emergency contact
- Keep mobile phone accessible but on silent mode
- Position yourself for quick response to coordination requests
- Prepare backup volunteers for potential emergency deployment

### Mid-Examination Monitoring (Every 60-90 Minutes)

**[ ] Discreet progress check** with examination supervisors
- Confirm examinations are proceeding smoothly
- Address any reported issues with scribe services
- Coordinate break procedures if applicable

**[ ] Emergency response readiness** for volunteer issues
- Monitor for signs of volunteer fatigue or stress
- Prepare replacement protocols for volunteer emergencies
- Maintain contact with backup volunteers throughout examination period

**[ ] Documentation preparation** for post-examination procedures
- Prepare feedback forms for distribution after examination completion
- Update attendance records and service hour tracking
- Document any issues or special circumstances for follow-up

## Post-Examination Procedures (Immediately After)

### Service Completion

**[ ] Confirm examination completion** and material submission
- Verify examination papers are properly submitted to administrators
- Confirm all accommodation requirements were met successfully
- Address any immediate concerns from students or volunteers

**[ ] Collect feedback** from both students and scribes
- Distribute post-examination evaluation forms
- Encourage honest feedback about service quality and coordination
- Document any suggested improvements or reported issues

**[ ] Complete attendance records** and service documentation
- Update volunteer service hours and attendance tracking
- Record examination completion and accommodation success
- Note any problems or special circumstances for programme records

### Immediate Follow-Up

**[ ] Thank volunteers** for their service contribution
- Acknowledge their time and effort professionally
- Provide information about service hour documentation
- Remind about upcoming training or volunteer opportunities

**[ ] Check with students** about accommodation satisfaction
- Confirm their needs were met effectively during examination
- Address any concerns or problems that arose
- Provide contact information for any follow-up needs

**[ ] Coordinate with examination administrators** for any needed follow-up
- Address any procedural issues or improvement suggestions
- Confirm satisfaction with coordination and volunteer performance
- Document feedback for future examination coordination

## End-of-Day Wrap-Up (Within 24 Hours)

### Data Recording and Analysis

**[ ] Update volunteer tracking database** with service records
- Record attendance, hours served, and performance notes
- Update student satisfaction and coordinator evaluation scores
- Document any issues or exceptional performance for recognition

**[ ] Process feedback forms** and identify improvement opportunities
- Analyse student and volunteer satisfaction ratings
- Identify common issues or suggestions for programme improvement
- Prepare summary reports for programme evaluation

**[ ] Follow up on any reported issues** or concerns
- Contact students or volunteers who reported problems
- Investigate and address any service quality concerns
- Implement immediate improvements to prevent recurring issues

### Communication and Recognition

**[ ] Send thank you messages** to all participating volunteers
- Acknowledge their contribution to student success
- Provide service hour confirmation for their records
- Include information about recognition opportunities or upcoming events

**[ ] Update programme stakeholders** on daily service delivery
- Provide summary statistics to disability services administration
- Report any issues or successes to institutional partners
- Update volunteer coordinators and training staff on performance trends

**[ ] Plan for next examination cycle** based on lessons learned
- Identify scheduling or coordination improvements needed
- Update volunteer availability and performance records
- Prepare for upcoming examination periods with improved procedures

## Emergency Protocols

### Volunteer No-Show or Cancellation

**Emergency Response (Within 15 minutes):**
1. **Contact backup volunteer** immediately using priority contact list
2. **Notify student** of potential delay while securing replacement
3. **Coordinate with exam administrator** for timing adjustments if needed
4. **Deploy backup volunteer** with briefing materials and support
5. **Document incident** for programme improvement and follow-up

### Student Accommodation Issues

**Immediate Response:**
1. **Assess accommodation needs** not being met by current arrangement
2. **Consult with student** about alternative solutions or modifications
3. **Coordinate with examination supervisor** for procedural adjustments
4. **Implement alternative accommodation** if available and appropriate
5. **Document situation** for institutional accommodation review

### Technical or Administrative Problems

**Response Procedure:**
1. **Identify problem scope** and impact on examination procedures
2. **Consult available resources** including backup materials and alternative arrangements
3. **Coordinate solution** with examination administrators and institutional support
4. **Implement workaround** that maintains accommodation effectiveness
5. **Follow up** with affected students to ensure satisfaction

---

**Checklist Duration:** 2-4 hours of coordination time per examination day
**Recommended Staffing:** 1 coordinator per 10-15 concurrent examinations
**Emergency Response Time:** Within 15 minutes for critical issues
**Documentation Required:** Attendance records, feedback forms, issue reports
