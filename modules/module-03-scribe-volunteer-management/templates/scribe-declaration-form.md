### Scribe Declaration

I, ________________________________ (Scribe Name), hereby solemnly declare and undertake that:

1. **Professional Conduct:** I will maintain the highest standards of professional conduct and integrity throughout the examination process.

2. **Transcription Accuracy:** I will write only and exactly what the student dictates to me, without any additions, omissions, corrections, or modifications of any kind.

3. **No Academic Assistance:** I will not provide any hints, suggestions, corrections, or academic assistance to the student in any form, whether verbal, written, or through body language.

4. **Confidentiality:** I will maintain strict confidentiality regarding:
   - All examination content and questions
   - Student responses and performance
   - Any personal information about the student
   - Any institutional procedures or policies

5. **No Discussion:** I will not discuss examination content, student performance, or any aspect of the examination with anyone during or after the examination.

6. **Independence:** I have no financial or other improper relationship with the student that would compromise my objectivity or the integrity of the examination process.

7. **Availability:** I am physically and mentally prepared to provide scribe services for the entire duration of the examination without breaks except as authorized by examination administrators.

8. **Technology Restrictions:** I will not use any electronic devices (mobile phones, calculators, etc.) during the examination except as specifically authorized by examination administrators.

9. **Compliance:** I will comply with all institutional policies, examination rules, and instructions from authorized examination personnel.

10. **Consequences Understanding:** I understand that any violation of these undertakings may result in:
    - Cancellation of the student's examination
    - Disciplinary action against me by the institution
    - Disqualification from future scribe services
    - Legal consequences if fraud or misconduct is established

11. **Voluntary Service:** I am providing this service voluntarily and have not been coerced or improperly induced to serve as a scribe.
